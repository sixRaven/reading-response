This is a reading response to

My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be?

I am especially interested in the narrative in which the author describes the duality of websites: “My favorite aspect of websites is their duality: they’re both subject and object at once.” And “When you put energy into a website, it turns the website helps from your own identity”

At the first glance, I thought it was something unnecessary to bring up. As we can always say “When you put energy into a painting/poem/calligraphy/etc., it turns the painting/poem/calligraphy/etc. helps from your own identity”

But later, I realized there is a reason to think that way. Thanks to the power of computation, website is far more flexible and interactive than any medium before. Therefore, a website can be a much more comprehensive representation of one’s identity. In addition, compared with fields such as painting, music, literature, making a website is more accessible to the majority of people and requires less specificity.

These words made me re-think about the meaning of a website of MINE and reminded me of the lecture about “stop drawing dead fish”- What is a live fish for you when it comes to create in the world of computation and internet? And what kind of fish are you?

Yuanhao Liu
